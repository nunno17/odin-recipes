This is how you make it

  1. Cut the onion
  2. Cut the carrots
  3. Cut the garlic
  4. Mix them and fry at high heat
  5. Let is simmer and add the tomato paste
  6. Add the mince meat
  7. Put the mix into the layers and right into the oven 180 degrees for 20 minutes!
